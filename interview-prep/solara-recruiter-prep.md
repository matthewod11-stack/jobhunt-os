## 8. Potential Landmines

- **"Why leave fintech for climate?"** The trap is answering it as asked. Jordan isn't leaving fintech: solar financing IS fintech, pointed at a better problem. Say that plainly, then stop. Do not perform climate zeal; one honest sentence about wanting the discipline pointed somewhere useful beats a conversion story.
- **The layoff.** One sentence per the framing rule. Over-explaining reads as shame.
- **No climate-industry experience.** Concede it fast, then name what transfers: regulated product, partner channels, a considered purchase. The installer ecosystem is learnable; say so plainly instead of pretending to know it.
- **Comp.** Climate Series B bands can sit below fintech bands. State the floor without hedging; if the band is below it, better to find out on this call.
- **"You've managed four people; this is a team of one."** Went zero-to-one at Copper & Pine before ever managing anyone. Lead with that, not with the team.
